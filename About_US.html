OUR BRANCHES

      

    

    



    
    

      

      
      

        
Colombo

        
Grocies Supermarket in Colombo is a modern hub for grocery shopping, offering a wide range of products from fresh produce to convenience foods. It combines Sri Lanka's rich agricultural heritage with contemporary retail practices, catering to the evolving needs of Colombo's residents.

        
      

    

    

      
      

        
Galle

        
Groceis Supermarket in Galle, Sri Lanka, is a modern grocery store that brings convenience and variety to the city's residents. Offering a wide selection of products from fresh produce to household essentials, it serves as a one-stop-shop for locals and visitors alike, combining the charm of Galle's historic ambiance with the efficiency of contemporary retail.

        
      

    

    
    

      
      

        
Kandy

        
Grocies Supermarket in Kandy, Sri Lanka, is a modern supermarket that offers a convenient and extensive selection of products, catering to the needs of the city's residents and visitors. Located in the heart of Kandy, it combines the city's rich cultural heritage with contemporary retail practices, providing a seamless shopping experience for everyone.
